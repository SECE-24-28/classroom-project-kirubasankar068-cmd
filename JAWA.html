JAWA 42 Bobber

A Factory-Custom Motorcycle Born From Retro Soul & Modern Craft

Definition

The JAWA 42 Bobber is a factory-custom motorcycle crafted with a unique blend of retro charisma and modern engineering. Its low-slung stance, sculpted tank, and single-seat design give it a bold presence, making it a distinctive bobber that stands apart on any road.

Specifications

  • Engine: 334cc Single Cylinder, Liquid Cooled
  • Power Output: 29.5 PS
  • Torque: 32.7 Nm
  • Transmission: 6-Speed Gearbox
  • Braking: Dual Disc with Dual-Channel ABS
  • Chassis: Double-Cradle Frame
  • Seat: Adjustable Single Seat
  • Design: Factory Custom Bobber Styling

Creator / Manufacturer

The JAWA 42 Bobber is produced by JAWA Motorcycles, revived in India by Classic Legends Pvt. Ltd., a Mahindra-owned company. The motorcycle merges the legacy of Czech design with modern Indian innovation.

Merits of JAWA 42 Bobber

  • Distinct factory-custom bobber design
  • Excellent mid-range torque performance
  • Comfortable riding posture
  • Premium build quality and finish
  • Retro aesthetics blended with contemporary tech
